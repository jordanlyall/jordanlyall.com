Personal infrastructure for running a fleet of specialized AI agents. Scout scans for signals, Researcher surfaces health and productivity content, Bodyguard monitors wallet security, Editor drafts content, Coach tracks personal metrics -- all coordinated through Slack with cross-agent handoffs.

Runs on an M2 Mac Mini via OpenClaw gateway. Multi-provider LLM fallback chain (OpenRouter, Gemini, OpenAI, Anthropic). Feedback loop learns from thumbs-up/down reactions to tune future output.
